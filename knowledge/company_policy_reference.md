# Chop Kong Hin Company and Policy Reference

Prepared on March 24, 2026 from official Chop Kong Hin webpages plus the
current project knowledge assets. Where the official site presents policy on a
product page or FAQ block, that detail should be treated as customer-facing
guidance but still rechecked on the live site if a dispute depends on it.

## Brand and company context

The official site presents Chop Kong Hin as a gold jewellery retailer with 916
and 999 gold product lines, gift-focused merchandising, engraving services, and
shipping across Malaysia. The homepage emphasizes that not all products are
listed online and invites customers to contact the company if they are looking
for items not shown on the site.

Customer-facing positioning visible on the site includes:

- jewellery categories such as rings, earrings, charms, pendants, necklaces,
  bracelets, bangles, anklets, and gold bars
- gift packaging options for special occasions
- custom engraving for selected items
- delivery and collection options

## Refund and exchange policy

According to the official refund and exchange policy page:

- Refund or exchange requests should be made within 7 days of receiving the
  item.
- Returned items are subject to verification and quality inspection.
- Processing after the returned goods are received typically takes 7 to 14
  working days.
- Returned items should be unworn, in original condition, and accompanied by
  the original packaging, certificates, and receipt.

### Engraved or customized items

The official policy says customized engravable items are non-refundable and
non-exchangeable because they are personalized to the customer's request.

### Non-customized items

The site describes non-engravable products as items sold in their original
form without personalization such as engraving, messages, or design changes.

### Exchange handling

The policy states:

- exchanges are allowed for items of equal or higher value
- if the replacement item is lower in value, the difference is compensated with
  store vouchers
- if the replacement item is higher in value, the customer pays the difference

### Refund methods

The official page describes two refund routes:

- full refund as store vouchers
- cash refund with a 10 percent deduction for administrative and handling costs

The policy also states refunds are issued only after inspection and approval.

### Non-returnable cases

The listed non-returnable or non-exchangeable cases include:

- customized or personalized items
- clearance or promotional purchases
- items worn or damaged through misuse

### Return shipping responsibility

The official policy says customers are responsible for return shipping unless
the item is confirmed to be defective or incorrect after inspection. The site
also recommends using a trackable shipping service and keeping proof of
postage.

## Shipping and fulfilment notes

The official site strongly promotes shipping across Malaysia. Based on current
official product-page FAQ content, customer-facing shipping information
includes:

- standard delivery in roughly 5 to 7 working days
- free standard shipping for orders of RM100 and above
- RM5 standard shipping for orders below RM100
- express courier at RM15
- store collection in roughly 1 to 3 working days at no charge

One product FAQ also states that standard orders are prepared after payment is
verified, shipped on Saturday, and typically arrive within 1 to 2 working days
after dispatch. Because that timing appears in a product FAQ snippet rather than
in a dedicated policy page, it should be treated as indicative rather than as a
guaranteed universal SLA.

## Gift packaging and engraving

The official site highlights premium gift packaging and personalized engraving
as part of the brand experience.

Current customer-facing guidance visible on the site includes:

- gift packaging can be added for special occasions
- engraving can be used for names, dates, messages, images, or drawings
- smaller items such as rings are better suited to words or simple artwork for
  clarity
- some gift-box promotions and complimentary gift-card messaging are shown on
  current product pages

## Company-policy guidance for future RAG

When answering policy questions, the RAG layer should prefer this order:

1. Official policy page for refund and exchange rules
2. Official homepage for high-level brand, service, and merchandising claims
3. Official product-page FAQ blocks for shipping or service details that are
   not yet present on dedicated policy pages

For sensitive customer disputes, the answer should:

- cite the official policy source
- avoid promising exceptions not stated on the site
- advise the user to contact Chop Kong Hin directly via WhatsApp or email when
  inspection or case-by-case handling is required
