====== PROJECT OVERVIEW ======
IMTool Improved is an internal Streamlit application for Chop Kong Hin. It is
used by staff to manage stock, purchases, sales, bookings, customers, salesmen,
barcode workflows, pattern mappings, and an internal AI assistant. The business
domain is jewellery inventory and sales, especially 916 and 999 gold items.
The main operational tables are stock, purchase, sale, booking, book_payment,
customer, and salesman.

====== ACCESS AND SECURITY ======
This is a staff-only system. There is no self-service account registration.
User accounts must be provisioned by authorized IT administrators. The app uses
Streamlit session state for login sessions and verifies passwords against the
users table. Bcrypt is the intended password model, and legacy plain-text
passwords are migrated after successful login. Every page calls require_auth()
before showing business data.

====== STOCK LIFECYCLE ======
The stock table is the center of the system. A stock item usually starts in
status IN STOCK. It can move to BOOKED through the booking flow or SOLD through
the sales flow. Stock rows store original purchase cost and later store booking
or sale pricing details. Important stock fields include stk_id, stk_type,
stk_pattern, stk_weight, stk_gold_type, stk_gold_cost, stk_labor_cost,
stk_status, stk_pur_id, stk_sale_id, stk_book_id, stk_barcode, stk_tag, and
stk_remark.

====== SALES WORKFLOW ======
One sale can contain multiple stock items. During sale creation, the app lets
staff pick multiple IN STOCK items and enter sell weight, gold sell price, and
labor sell for each item. When the sale is confirmed, the code locks each stock
row with FOR UPDATE, verifies that it is still IN STOCK, creates a sale header,
and marks each linked stock item as SOLD. Profit is calculated per stock item
from sell revenue minus original cost.

====== BOOKING WORKFLOW ======
One booking can contain multiple stock items. The booking page allows staff to
select multiple IN STOCK items, enter per-item booking pricing, and collect an
optional initial deposit. The booking header stores aggregate totals such as
book_price and book_remaining, while item-level booking pricing is written onto
the stock rows. Additional payments are stored in the book_payment table. When
a booking is closed, the app locks the booking and linked stock rows, adds any
final payment needed, marks the booking as COMPLETED, creates a sale record,
and converts the booked stock rows to SOLD in one atomic flow.

====== DATABASE SHAPE ======
The live schema name is konghin. Main business tables are users, customer,
salesman, purchase, stock, sale, booking, book_payment, category_pattern_mapping,
and metadata. There are currently no physical foreign key constraints in the
live schema, so relationships are enforced mainly by application logic. Logical
join paths include stock.stk_pur_id to purchase.pur_id, stock.stk_sale_id to
sale.sale_id, stock.stk_book_id to booking.book_id, sale.sale_cust_id to
customer.cust_id, booking.book_cust_id to customer.cust_id, purchase.pur_slm_id
to salesman.slm_id, and book_payment.bp_book_id to booking.book_id.

====== DATA INTEGRITY AND CONCURRENCY ======
The system uses explicit database transactions for critical writes. Generic CRUD
editing now checks the original last_update timestamp before saving so the app
can reject stale edits instead of silently overwriting another user's changes.
Sales and bookings use SELECT FOR UPDATE on stock or booking rows before
changing them. This means critical inventory actions are designed to be safer
when multiple staff are using the system at the same time.

====== CACHING AND PERFORMANCE ======
The app caches the DatabaseManager singleton and also caches selected read
queries through shared helpers. Main CRUD pages now use SQL pagination and
server-side counts instead of loading full tables first. Caches are cleared
after successful writes so screens do not keep stale data longer than needed.

====== CURRENT AI ASSISTANT ======
The Agentic Intelligence page uses a four-stage pipeline. First, a question
understanding agent decides whether a question is general or database-related.
If it is general, the assistant retrieves relevant chunks from this
knowledge.txt file. If it is database-related, a SQL agent generates read-only
SQL, a data evaluation agent checks result quality, and a summary agent writes
the final answer. The SQL agent uses a separate schema guide file named
Bilingual README.txt for grounding.

====== COMPANY OVERVIEW ======
Chop Kong Hin's official website presents the company as a gold jewellery
retailer with 916 and 999 gold product lines, gift-focused presentation, and
engraving services. The homepage promotes free shipping across Malaysia and
also notes that not all products are listed online. Customers are encouraged to
contact the company if they are searching for items not shown on the website.

====== REFUND AND EXCHANGE POLICY ======
Based on the official Chop Kong Hin refund and exchange policy page, refund or
exchange requests should be made within 7 days from the date the customer
receives the item. Returned items are inspected after being shipped back to the
company. The inspection and processing period typically takes 7 to 14 working
days after the goods are received. Returned items should be unworn, in original
condition, and accompanied by original packaging, certificates, and receipt.

====== CUSTOMIZED ITEM POLICY ======
The official policy states that customized engravable items are non-refundable
and non-exchangeable because they are personalized to the customer's request.
For non-customized products, the site describes them as items sold in their
original form without personalization or design alteration.

====== REFUND METHODS AND EXCHANGE RULES ======
The official policy describes two refund methods: full refund as store vouchers
or a cash refund with a 10 percent deduction for administrative and handling
costs. Exchanges are allowed for items of equal or higher value. If the
replacement item is lower in value, the difference is compensated with store
vouchers. If the replacement item is higher in value, the customer pays the
difference. Customers are responsible for return shipping unless the item is
confirmed to be defective or incorrect after inspection.

====== SHIPPING, GIFTING, AND ENGRAVING ======
Current official site content highlights delivery across Malaysia, premium gift
packaging, and custom engraving for selected items. Product-page FAQ content
mentions standard delivery, express courier, and store collection options.
Current customer-facing examples mention free standard shipping for orders of
RM100 and above, a lower-fee option for smaller orders, and express courier as
a paid upgrade. The site also presents engraving for names, dates, messages,
images, or drawings, with simpler designs recommended for smaller items such as
rings.
